About TerpInsider

What is TerpInsider?

TerpInsider is a web application built to help students discover clubs and organizations on campus, compare them by category, view realistic meeting locations on a map, and read reviews from other students.

Target Users

University of Maryland students who want to find clubs that match their interests, especially new students exploring campus life or upperclassmen looking for new organizations.

Nala – AI Club Advisor

The home page features Nala an AI chatbot powered by OpenAI that recommends clubs from our Supabase database based on what you describe (interests, location, category, and more).

Project Goals

  • Centralize club discovery in one searchable directory
  • Make it easy to filter clubs by category
  • Show meeting locations using maps (OpenStreetMap + Leaflet)
  • Allow students to leave and view reviews with rating charts
  • Eventually support AI-powered club recommendations (future roadmap)

Technologies

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Fetch API), Leaflet.js, Chart.js
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • External API: OpenStreetMap Nominatim (geocoding)
  • Deployment: Vercel
